About Me

Ashley Kohl Adler is a leading innovator in the world of dance education. Trained in classical ballet at Atlanta Ballet, Ashley received a BFA from The Boston Conservatory of Dance. Ashley has performed on stages all through the world, including Lincoln Center and Madison Square Garden in NYC. In her post performing career, Ashley has built a reputation as an innovative leader in dance education, working with dance schools across the country, most recently at the Colorado Ballet Academy in Denver, Colorado.
